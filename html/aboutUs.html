Who We Are

Piscis Aquarium is an animal welfare organization based in Jakarta, Indonesia. We are dedicated to preserving marine life all over the world. Our main mission is to raise 500 million USD by 2030 in order to clean up the Great Pacific Garbage Patch by 2050. We also aim to educate people around the world about marine life to promote our cause. Piscis Aquarium is open to the public in 30 countries around the world, including the United States, England, Brazil, South Africa, Japan and many more!
